Privacy policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Sail Halong publishes travel guides and links to third-party booking and source websites. We do not sell cruises, process payments or ask visitors to create an account.

Information collected on this site

Sail Halong does not provide user accounts, comments, contact forms or checkout. We do not ask for your name, email address, booking reference or payment details.

Google Analytics and live GetYourGuide activity widgets remain completely unloaded unless you select “Accept analytics.” If you accept, Google Analytics measures page visits, sessions and clicks from Sail Halong to GetYourGuide, together with aggregated information such as device category, country and traffic source. GetYourGuide requests current product information and may receive technical data such as IP address, browser details and the referring page, and create session identifiers for attribution. Sail Halong keeps Google advertising signals, ad storage and personalization disabled; GetYourGuide handles its widget data under its own privacy policy.

Your choice is stored in your browser so we can respect it on later pages. Analytics may set first-party measurement cookies only after acceptance. Sail Halong configures the shortest practical user-level retention and does not send names, email addresses, booking references or text entered by visitors.

Our hosting provider may process standard technical request data needed to deliver and protect the site, such as an IP address, browser type, requested page and time of access. That processing is governed by the provider's own terms and privacy practices.

Affiliate and external links

Some links are affiliate links. Their addresses include parameters that identify Sail Halong as the referring publisher. If you follow one, the third-party website may process your visit, use cookies or similar technology, and attribute a later booking according to its own privacy policy. Sail Halong may receive a commission if you book, at no extra cost to you.

Links to booking providers, official sources and other websites take you outside Sail Halong. The destination website controls what it collects and how it uses that information. Review its privacy policy before submitting personal or payment details.

Payments and personal details

Sail Halong does not receive card numbers, booking details or payment information. Any booking and payment take place on the provider's website. We do not sell personal information.

Your choices

You can accept or decline analytics when the notice appears. Both choices leave the full editorial site and the existing booking links available. If you decline, live activity cards remain unloaded. Use “Privacy choices” in the footer to change your decision later. Revoking consent removes Sail Halong’s Google Analytics cookies and reloads the page without Analytics.

You can also decline to follow external links and use browser controls to block or clear storage set by this or other websites. Because Sail Halong has no accounts or forms and does not send a user ID to Analytics, it does not keep an identifiable visitor profile for you to access through this site.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page before adding forms, accounts, advertising, another analytics provider or any feature that materially changes how visitor data is handled. The date above will show the latest revision.

Scope

This page describes the current technical implementation of Sail Halong. It is general information and is not legal advice.
