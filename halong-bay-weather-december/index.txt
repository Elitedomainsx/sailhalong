1b:T686,<h2 id="halong-bay-temperatures-in-december-the-numbers-checked-july-2026">Halong Bay temperatures in December: the numbers (checked July 2026)</h2>

<p>December is the driest and lowest-humidity month in the normals. That does not make it the clearest: the <em>crachin</em> season begins in late November and peaks in February. Rainfall, humidity and visibility are different measurements.</p>
<p>Daylight is not sunshine. December gives about 10.8 hours between sunrise and sunset, but only 4.1 hours of measured sunshine per day on average. A dry day can still spend hours under cloud or mist.</p>

24:T903,<h2 id="what-december-weather-does-to-a-halong-bay-cruise">What December weather does to a Halong Bay cruise</h2>
<p>The short day changes the format calculation. A <a href="/halong-bay-day-trips-from-hanoi/">day trip from Hanoi</a> typically lasts 12 to 13 hours door to door, with 4 to 5 hours on the highway. An <a href="/halong-bay-overnight-cruises/">overnight cruise</a> keeps you in the bay after a December sunset and through the following morning. Exact schedules vary, so verify the departure, return and route when you book.</p>
<p>Sunrise moves from about 6:11 a.m. at the start of December to 6:28 a.m. at the end; sunset shifts from 5:09 to 5:20 p.m. If morning or evening light is the reason you are choosing a format, compare those times with the seller&#39;s current schedule rather than assuming the word “overnight” guarantees both.</p>
<p>The recurring winter review is not about rain. It is the traveler who packed for “Vietnam equals tropics” and met a 15.3°C morning on an open deck.</p>
<p>The cold is manageable with layers. A T-shirt may work around the 21.7°C average high; the deck before breakfast calls for a fleece or light jacket. Add a wind-resistant outer layer and closed shoes with grip. That is the article&#39;s one packing rule worth remembering.</p>
<p>The 21.7°C average sea temperature makes swimming a matter of cold tolerance, not a reliable December plan. Check the day&#39;s conditions and whether your route offers a swimming stop. Kayaking also depends on the actual wind, waves and operator decision. Cruise brochures have mastered the infinity-pool photo at golden hour; in December, golden hour is around 4:40 p.m. and the pool is mostly for looking at.</p>
<p>December sits outside the main late-May-to-autumn tropical-cyclone planning window, but no month is storm-proof. The latest <a href="https://www.nchmf.gov.vn/kttv/vi-VN/1/ban-tin-du-bao-khi-hau-thoi-han-nam-tren-pham-vi-toan-quoc-tu-thang-8-nam-2026-den-thang-7-nam-2027-post52559.html">NCHMF annual outlook</a> still warns of strong marine winds from tropical systems and cold-air outbreaks. There is no reliable public cancellation percentage. Check the <a href="https://www.nchmf.gov.vn/kttv/">official NCHMF forecast</a> and the seller&#39;s current terms close to departure.</p>

25:T4a0,<h2 id="who-december-is-for-and-who-should-skip-it">Who December is for, and who should skip it</h2>
<p>Winter Halong and summer Halong are different trips, not better and worse versions of one trip. December&#39;s normals are 15.3 to 21.7°C, 18.1 mm of rain and a 21.7°C sea. July runs 26.2 to 31.7°C, 364.3 mm of rain and a 30.5°C sea. You are choosing between cooler sightseeing conditions and warmer water with much more rain.</p>
<p>December fits if your priorities are the limestone scenery, photography, caves and cooler deck time, and if possible mist will not spoil the trip. If you are still deciding whether the bay belongs on your route at all, our <a href="/is-halong-bay-worth-visiting/">is Halong Bay worth visiting</a> guide takes a side.</p>
<p>Skip December if warm-water swimming and hot weather are central to the trip. If fog would ruin the view for you, remember that the <em>crachin</em> season has already begun even though its peak comes later. December&#39;s low rainfall improves the odds of a dry day; it does not guarantee visibility. Our <a href="/halong-bay-weather-best-time/">Halong Bay weather and best time guide</a> compares all 12 months.</p>
